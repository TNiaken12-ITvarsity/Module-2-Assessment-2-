Checkers

Checkers is a South African supermarket chain founded in 1933. It is the largest supermarket chain in South Africa by market share. Checkers stores offer a wide variety of groceries, household goods, and other products. They also have a number of in-store departments, such as bakeries, delis, and pharmacies.

Monday to Friday from 9 am to 6 pm, Saturday from 8 am to 6 pm, and Sunday from 9 am to 5 pm. The phone number for the store is 011 831 6860.
