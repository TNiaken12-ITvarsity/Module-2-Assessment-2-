Spur

Spur is a South African steakhouse franchise restaurant chain popular for its family-friendly atmosphere

Their menu centers around steaks, but also includes:

  • Burgers
  • Chicken dishes
  • Ribs
  • Seafood options
  • Salads and vegetarian choices
  • A dedicated kid's menu
