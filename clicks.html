Clicks

When referring to "Clicks" in South Africa, it likely refers to Clicks Pharmacy, a retail chain focusing on health and beauty products.

Clicks offers a wide range of health and beauty products, including:

  • Over-the-counter medications: Pain relievers, allergy meds, cough and cold syrups, etc.
  • Vitamins and supplements
  • Beauty products: Skincare, makeup, haircare, fragrances
  • Toiletries: Deodorant, toothpaste, soap, etc.
  • Baby care products
